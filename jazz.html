Why Jazz Is My Hobby

Jazz is the music I return to when I want to feel something — a place where improvisation meets deep feeling, and every listening session tells a new story.

Emotion & Atmosphere

Jazz creates moods — late-night introspection, warm afternoons, or a bright morning with coffee. The colors, harmonies, and phrasing evoke emotions I relate to.

Discovery & Storytelling

I love the hunt — finding obscure records, reading liner notes, and tracing influences across artists and eras. Each record is a story and a place to explore.

Improvisation & Creativity

Jazz celebrates spontaneity. Listening is active — I follow solos, predict phrases, and appreciate moments that could never repeat exactly the same way.

My Listening Ritual

Time & Place

I often listen in the evening with a single album on repeat, or during quiet weekend mornings while reading. The setting shapes the experience.

Format

Vinyl when I can, high-quality streaming for discovery. I love the tactile act of cueing a record and the warmth it brings.

Playlist

I keep a small, evolving playlist of favorites — classics, recent discoveries, and tracks that feel like companions.
