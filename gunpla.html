Keep consistent distance (~10‚Äì20 cm), keep the gun moving, and use multiple thin passes. Clean thoroughly between colors and always test spray on scrap materials.

Masking tips ‚ñæ

Use high-quality low-tack tape, press edges firmly, and leave the tape on until the paint is fully cured to avoid lifting edges.

Weathering recipes ‚ñæ

Simple wash: dark enamel thinned heavily; apply to recesses and wick away excess. Pigments: mix with water/alcohol binder; dab and fix with matte sealer. Chipping: sponge or hairspray base-coat method for realistic edges.
